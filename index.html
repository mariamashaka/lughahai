How it works

Built by communities

Native speakers, educated moderators and learners working together.

🗣️

Contributors

Native speakers record words, phrases and audio — filling in structured templates for each topic.

✅

Moderators

Educated native speakers review contributions, select the best audio and compile grammar references.

📖

Learners

Anyone can learn any language for free — with structured lessons, audio and grammar references.
